Crystal Collector

Gameplay:


  • The computer will randomly chose the score at the start of each game.
  • Each of the four crystals below will be randomly assigned a value which will be hidden from you.
  • Your goal is to click on the crystals as needed so that your score matches, but does not exceed, the computer's score. If you go over, you lose.
  • Each time the game starts, the crystal values and the score you have to match are changed.
